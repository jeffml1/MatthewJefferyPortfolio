About Me

I am a B.I.T. student who also works part-time as a web developer at The Logic Studio Ltd. I have a keen interest in computer programming, particularly Java and Web Development. Previously, I have interned at Tracmap and Abacus Biotechnology.

At The Logic Studio I develop websites for a variety of clients, the main languages I use are HTML5, CSS3, PHP, JavaScript and jQuery. I also do some work with the Silverstripe CMS.

At Tracmap, I built a progressive web application to be bundled into iOS and Android apps, using Cordova Phone Gap. Here, I was mainly focused on backend Javascript, using Node.js.

At Abacus Biotechnology, I reviewed and analysed various online databases that could be applied to commercial bee keeping industries. In this position, I largely worked with the farm management system, FarmIQ.

On this blog, I will document and share my IT research and other interesting things I find regarding IT. I loves to hear from my readers, and always appreciates your feedback.
